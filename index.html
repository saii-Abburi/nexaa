About NEXAA

NEXAA (Next Gen Engineers & AI Association) is the central technical authority of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning).

Formed to unify, govern, and elevate all technical efforts within the department, NEXAA functions as a year-round, structured body rather than an event-specific organization. Every technical initiative ‚Äî including hackathons, workshops, coding competitions, research activities, and innovation programs ‚Äî operates under the NEXAA framework.

NEXAA is responsible for the complete lifecycle of technical activities: strategic planning, execution, coordination, evaluation, and continuous improvement. It ensures that all programs align with industry trends, academic goals, and emerging technologies in AI and machine learning.

Beyond events, NEXAA drives sustained skill development, leadership grooming, peer learning, and industry‚Äìacademia exposure through expert talks, mentorship programs, collaborative projects, and technology partnerships.
